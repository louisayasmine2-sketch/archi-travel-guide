## 🔄 CARA CONVERT FOTO KE .WEBP

### Opsi 1 — Online squoosh.app (paling mudah)
1. Buka https://squoosh.app
2. Upload fotomu (`hero-venice-tuscany-day-trip.jpg`)
3. Di kanan pilih format WebP
4. Set quality ke 82
5. Download → rename menjadi `hero-venice-tuscany-day-trip.webp`

### Opsi 2 — Online batch
1. Buka https://convertio.co/jpg-webp/
2. Upload foto → convert → download
